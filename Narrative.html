Narrative Building

Moana

This project recreates the powerful manta ray scene from Moana, capturing its emotional depth and symbolic meaning. I focused on distilling the moment’s essence movement, transformation, and light then abstracted these elements into a simplified visual language. The final outcome reflects the scene’s emotion without directly replicating it, transforming narrative cinema into expressive, conceptual design.

Mindmap

To begin, I studied the scene closely and pulled out its core meaning and emotional sequence. By breaking down the visuals, mood, and symbolism, I identified the elements that truly drive the moment, forming the foundation for the abstraction process.

Character sketchs

I abstracted the character by reducing it to its simplest geometric forms a triangle for the body and a circle for the head. This minimal approach lets the emotion and essence of the scene stand out without relying on literal representation.

Color Palette

The color palette plays a key role in conveying the scene’s emotional depth. Turquoise represents healing and clarity, while cyan adds a spiritual and magical quality. White brings in purity, guidance, and cultural connection. Earthy red symbolizes courage and grounding. Indigo reflects despair and hopelessness, capturing the darker emotional undertones of the moment.
